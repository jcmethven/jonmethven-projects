Various Rooms In Trump Tower That Day

A man discovers the donuts in the executive lounge are gone. He spends the morning searching the building directory for Hispanic names and anonymously reporting them to immigration.

23rd Floor, Room 11

A woman gives a presentation to colleagues, all of whom stare at their phones. Most look at pornography or MMA highlights.

12th Floor, Room 14

Two children cannot find their mother. People dressed in business casual laugh and point at them.

5th Floor, Room 8

Six Melania lookalikes sit around a table. No one has told them why they are there. A man in a suit checks on them every hour but says nothing.

3rd Floor, Room 1

Building security interrogates a housekeeper. From massage tables naked execs debate the logic of why a housekeeper would steal hundreds of donuts, though all agree she's guilty.

Executive Lounge

Two men sit at desks. They only communicate with each other through hand puppets. A woman furiously searches the job ads.

15th Floor, Room 2

A man smashes his fist into the paper towel dispenser because it’s empty. A second man gives himself a pep talk in the mirror. The washroom attendant is on the phone with his bookie.

17th Floor, Men's Room

People sit around a table spitting at a picture of Hillary Clinton. The table is littered with donuts. Each donut has one bite missing.

25th Floor, Secret Conference Room

A woman speaks to Chinese businessmen. An interpreter repeats it in Mandarin. A sign language interpreter translates. A dance interpreter pirouettes. Behind them, a sound technician naps.

18th Floor, Conference Room

ICE agents shut down the lobby having received dozens of calls of immigrant sightings. In custody are three security officers and a pregnant tourist who just stopped to take a picture.

Lobby

A man, clutching a picture frame, weeping. A woman trying to snare a pigeon on the windowsill with her iPhone charger.

33rd Floor, Room 20

A dark room, an empty desk. On the corner of the desk, a copy of “Fire and Fury: Inside the Trump White House,” nearly two years before publication. Nearby, a man chain-vapes.

22nd Floor, Room 14

An actual McDonald’s franchise. Only a few people know it exists, though a staff of 15 is expected to have the full menu hot and ready 24/7. Most food is cooked and thrown away.

7th Floor, Room 6

Producers, lawyers and Bruce Willis discuss a Die Hard set in Trump Tower. All agree Donald Trump will have a cameo, though the lawyers insist he shoot a terrorist. Bruce Willis is texting.

44th Floor, Room 22

A doomsday bunker well stocked with artillery, Diet Coke, and the entire Stormy Daniels oeuvre.

9th Floor, Room 5

A man and woman stare at the Hudson as ships pass. On Spotify, Air Supply’s Two Less Lonely People. They meet eyes and know, for certain, this is the worst moment of their careers.

47th Floor, Room 11

Room without furniture. Chris Christie sitting on the floor, a KFC family meal laid out, awaiting a lunch guest.

35th Floor, Room 3

FBI sting operation.
